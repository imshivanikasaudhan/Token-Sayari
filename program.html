Our Programs

High Yields Program

Project Sayari LTD will establish a $10,000,000 investment pool, offering investors security tokens (Deluxe Token, SDX) at an exchange rate of 1 SDX = $10. A minimum investment of $100,000 is required, with a smart contract on the advanced PROTON blockchain managing the pool and issuance of SDX tokens. Investors will receive a unique username and account to track their tokens.The 40-week program will start within 45 days of investment submission, or earlier if the pool fills up sooner. A professional investment team will manage the pool, targeting a 5% weekly profit. If the pool is not filled in 45 days, investments will receive a 50% annual return until the program begins. Investors will be notified via email and the sayari-planet.com website.Investors will receive a weekly profit, distributed in fiat currency, based on the number of SDX tokens held.At the program’s end, investors have the option to sell their tokens back to Project Sayari at the original exchange rate (1 SDX = $10), or reinvest for another 40 weeks under the same conditions.In summary, this 40-week investment program provides a secure, profitable opportunity for investors. Blockchain technology and smart contracts ensure transparency and security, with a weekly profit and flexible options for reinvestment or token buyback.

Loyalty Program

This blockchain based loyalty program, is a smart contracts based program whereby the buyer receives the SAYT token as a discount on purchases or transactions made with Planet Sayari’s partners, using the Sayari loyalty card. All other loyalty programs on the market can be matched and linked to the SAYT token.Members of the Planet Sayari’s loyalty program are eligible to receive discounts from merchants or business partners that the company has negotiated with through the use of Planet Sayari’s tokens. As part of the Sayari loyalty program, consumers can earn more bonus tokens by enrolling in the program, referring others, and staking the token for a certain period of time.How it works:Earn SAYT tokens for every purchase or transaction completed at our commercial and financial partners, using our SAYT loyalty card. SAYT loyalty card is equipped with a QR code that is scanned and gives loyalty tokens to the buyer.Turn your SAYT tokens into fiat currency and expand your shopping possibilities. Spend your rewards however you like.
